Can't Sign in?

Original Alpha Tester

  1. Refresh the Alpha signin page, here
  2. Double check credentials and try again
  3. Email us at support@cyphor.io


Invited Alpha Tester

  1. Refresh the page
  2. Double check credentials and try again
  3. Confirm your friend has invited you to the alpha via invite a friend
  4. Email us at support@cyphor.io

Download doesn't work?

Original Alpha Tester

  1. Try to access the Cyphor App directly here. If this does not work please follow the steps below.
  2. Make sure you are signed in to your Chrome Browser with a personal account and not an enterprise managed account. Chrome Browser accounts tutorial here.
  3. Try step 1 again, email us at support@cyphor.io


Invited Alpha Tester

  1. Confirm your friend has invited you to the alpha via invite a friend.
  2. Try the steps for original Alpha Testers
  3. Email us at support@cyphor.io
